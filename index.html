Vertafore

Modern Insurance Company

Vertafore is a modern insurance technology company with headquarters in East Lansing, Mi. They sell products from agency solutions, management systems, sales tools, and much more. They work towards making companies as efficient as they can get. With all of their software, a company can reach limits they would never think was possible. Whether it’s a small company or large company, management software will always apply.

Comprehensive agency solutions:

They just recently released their Vertafore Agency Platform, which solves challenging business problems. It is recognized to “retain customers, maximize profitability, and successfully manage all line of business.” This includes being able to customize the look of it to see all your tasks for the day, access to the industry’s leading real-time rater, searching for clients, and able to send documents through it.

Management systems:

The AMS360 system can put all policy information and even client information into the hands of the company to ensure customer service satisfaction. This is boasted to be the best insurance agency management system that is in the current market.

Content management and workflow:

Their WorkSmart software enables the user to reach goals without having to hire new employees to fill the roles. It conducts utilizing data into actions, it can send the work to the right person which can reduce steps in the process, and can even look ahead for any potential problems.
